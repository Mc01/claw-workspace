Mission

What is ChamaFi?

ChamaFi digitizes existing Chama savings groups across Africa — bringing the trust of community finance onto the Celo blockchain.

300 million+ Africans participate in savings circles (Chamas, Susus, Tontines). These informal groups work — but they're limited by cash, geography, and trust. ChamaFi keeps the human element while replacing the limitations with smart contracts.

Process

Five Steps to Graduate

From creation to yield — powered by smart contracts on Celo.

01

Create or Join a Chama

Start a new savings circle or join an existing one. Set your contribution amount, member cap, and deadline. Every rule is encoded in a smart contract — immutable and transparent.

Smart Contract Deployed
02

Contribute Capital

Each member deposits their share in cUSD. During incubation, pooled capital earns yield through DeFi rehypothecation — your savings work while you wait.

Capital Earns Yield
03

Graduate Instantly at 100%

Once the Chama is fully funded, it graduates automatically. No waiting, no admin approval. Missed deadline? All contributions are auto-refunded — no questions asked.

Instant Graduation
04

Earn LP Tokens + Yield

On graduation, members receive LP tokens representing their share of the pool. These LP tokens continue earning yield from rehypothecation — your stake keeps growing.

LP Tokens Issued
05

Exit Anytime

Two exit paths: call remove_liquidity to redeem LP tokens for your cUSD plus yield, or sell your LP tokens on the bonding curve for instant liquidity. Full sovereignty, zero lockup.

No Lockup

The Mechanics

Under the hood — how ChamaFi actually works on-chain.

Phase 1

Incubation

Members join and contribute capital. The pool is deployed into yield-bearing DeFi positions via rehypothecation — earning while the Chama fills up.

💰 Capital pooled in smart contract
📈 Rehypothecated for yield
⏰ Deadline enforced on-chain
⚠ Miss deadline → auto-refund
100% filled
Phase 2

Graduation

When fully funded, the contract graduates instantly — no admin needed. LP tokens are minted and distributed to each member proportional to their contribution.

🎉 Instant graduation, no delay
📋 LP tokens minted & distributed
📈 Yield accumulation continues
LP tokens in hand
Phase 3

Exit

LP holders choose how to exit. No lockup, no permission required — full sovereignty over your capital.

Option A
remove_liquidity Redeem LP → cUSD + accrued yield
OR
Option B
sell on bonding curve Sell LP tokens to buyers instantly
🔄

Rehypothecation

While incubating, pooled cUSD is deployed into Celo DeFi protocols. The yield accrues to LP token holders — traditional chamas earn 0% on idle capital.

🍵

Bonding Curve

LP tokens trade on an automated bonding curve. The curve provides instant liquidity without requiring a counterparty — exit any time at a fair price.

🔐

Auto-Refund

If a Chama fails to reach 100% before its deadline, all contributions are automatically returned to depositors. Zero loss, zero admin, zero trust required.

📋

LP Token Yield

LP tokens are productive assets — a claim on the pool plus ongoing yield from protocol fees and rehypothecation. Hold them or sell them — the math works either way.
