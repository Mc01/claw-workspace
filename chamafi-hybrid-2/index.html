How It Works

From zero to yield-generating savings circle in five steps.

01
Join or Create

Create or Join a Chama

Open ChamaFi in MiniPay, on the web, or in Farcaster. Name your circle, set target size and contribution amount, then invite members with a shareable link — or join an existing one.

02
Incubation Phase

Contribute Capital

Members contribute cUSD into the shared pool. The Chama is in Incubation until the target capital is reached. Funds earn yield from day one via rehypothecation.

03
Graduation

Graduate Instantly at 100%

When the pool hits its target, the Chama graduates automatically. No waiting, no admin approval — the smart contract handles everything on-chain.

04
LP Tokens

Earn LP Tokens + Yield

Upon graduation, members receive LP tokens proportional to their contribution. These tokens represent your share of the pool and accrue yield from the underlying DeFi strategy.

05
Exit Anytime

Exit Anytime

No lockups. Redeem LP tokens via remove_liquidity to withdraw your share + accrued yield, or sell your LP tokens on the bonding curve for instant liquidity — your choice, your timeline.

Auto-Refund on Missed Deadline

If a Chama fails to reach its target capital before the deadline, the smart contract automatically refunds all contributors in full — no admin needed, no loss of funds.

contract.refundAll() ← triggered on deadline

Yield via Rehypothecation

LP tokens held in the pool are deployed to Celo's DeFi ecosystem (e.g. Mento, Uniswap V3) to earn yield. Accrued yield is reflected in LP token value — no manual claiming needed.

pool → DeFi strategy → LP value ↑

Bonding Curve Exit

Don't want to wait? Sell your LP tokens on the embedded bonding curve for instant cUSD. The curve maintains price stability and ensures there's always a buyer — no counterparty needed.

LP tokens → bonding curve → cUSD

Remove Liquidity Exit

Prefer to withdraw your full share? Call remove_liquidity to burn your LP tokens and receive your contribution plus all accrued yield — principal protected, gains included.

remove_liquidity() → principal + yield
